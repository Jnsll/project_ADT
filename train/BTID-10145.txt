Candidatus Blochmannia pennsylvanicus str. BPEN
Description
Candidatus Blochmannia pennsylvanicus str. BPEN. This endosymbiont lives in ants of the genus Camponotus. This microbe lives within host cells (bacteriocytes and ant ovaries) and is passed to offspring via vertical transmission. The genome shows a low GC content and accelerated rate of evolution and gene reduction.
